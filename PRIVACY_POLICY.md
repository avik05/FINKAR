## 2. Storage & Security

## 3. Financial Data Protection

- We implement industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your information.

## 5. Your Rights
You have the right to access, edit, or delete any of your data and information at any time from within the dashboard settings.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or laws.
